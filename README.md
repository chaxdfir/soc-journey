# soc-journey
My journey into SOC, including labs, notes and log analysis practice.


## About SOC
The Security Operations Center (SOC) is the first line of defense against cyber threats. It focuses on detecting, analyzing, and responding to security incidents to protect systems and data.

## Responsibilities
- Monitor and investigate security alerts
- Analyze potential threats and suspicious activity
- Collaborate with other teams to improve security
- Continuously learn about new attacks and defense techniques

## SOC Roles
- L1 Analysts: Junior members who triage alerts and pass complex cases to L2
- L2 Analysts: Experienced members who investigate more advanced attacks
- Engineers: Experts in configuring security tools 
- Manager: A person who manages the whole team

## Specialized Roles
- Threat Intelligence Analyst: Gather data about emerging threat groups
- AppSec Engineer: Maintain a secure software development lifecycle
- AI Researcher: Study AI threats and how to defend against them
- Digital Forensics Analyst: Uncover hidden threats in disk and memory

## SOC vs MSSP

SOC is an internal team that handles security monitoring and incident response.

MSSP is an external provider that offers security services to organizations.

## Goal
To build a strong foundation in SOC and cybersecurity.
